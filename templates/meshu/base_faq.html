frequently asked questions

What are the holiday ordering deadlines?

Each meshu requires custom manufacturing, so unfortunately they require some lead time.

Silver, Brass and Nylon: Nov. 25th

Bamboo and Acrylic: Dec. 8th

Print Maps: Dec. 15th

The above dates are for US orders. For all other countries, it's hard to tell how long shipping will take, please subtract another 1-2 weeks for international shipping.

Where do you ship?

We ship all over the world!

How long does it take to make a meshu?

It depends on the material! Silver and brass pieces take 2-3 weeks to manufacturer. Bamboo, acrylic, and nylon take 1-2 weeks. All prints ship in under one week.

Where are meshus made?

Each meshu is manufactured in the USA, then hand-assembled and shipped from Los Angeles.
