We are Sha and Rachel and we like you.

Our ability to make custom pieces is only possible because of the wonderful Nicole Aptekar and the from-the-future-duo of Ponoko and Shapeways.

Sha Hwang

Sha studied and worked in architecture before working on maps and data visualizations at Stamen. Currently at Trulia, Sha continues to explore his interests in the flows of cities. Sha mostly just tweets, but tries to pin from time to time.

Rachel Binx

Rachel's background is in math and art history, but has also been making jewelry in some form or another since she was a kid. She is currently freelancing as a data visualizer, and spends her nights thinking about internet culture and animated GIFs. Rachel tweets, flicks, and occasionally pins.
