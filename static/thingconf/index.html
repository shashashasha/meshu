ThingConf!

Hi hello welcome to a conference about making and selling things online.

Buy Your Ticket Here!

It's getting easier and easier to start a small business online, and put your product directly in the hands of people around the world. This one-day conference aims to bring together people who are working in this space, to share ideas and learn some new tricks.

We're going to focus on four topics:

  • Manufacturing
    • Prototyping Resources
    • Small-Run Manufacturers
    • Scaling up
  • Selling
    • Marketplace vs. Custom Site
    • Kickstarter
    • Payment Processors
  • Fulfilling
    • Packaging
    • Shipping
    • Customer Service
  • Marketing
    • Launching your Product
    • Social Media
    • Press Kit

    Rachel Binx is a data visualizer, developer, designer, and mathematician. She has co-founded Meshu and Gifpop, two small companies that explore creating one-off physical objects from the data that each person finds meaningful. Previously, she worked as a design technologist for Stamen Design, where she built projects for clients such as MTV, Facebook, and Oprah. In general, she's a fan of anything that converts javascript to custom physical objects.
